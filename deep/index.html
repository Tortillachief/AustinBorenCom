Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Deep handles information when you use our iOS application.

No Data Collection

Deep does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data generated by your use of the app remains exclusively on your device.

Local Storage Only

Deep stores the following preferences locally on your device: card viewing options, hidden cards, theme selection (colorful or grayscale), welcome message display status, and shuffle mode preference. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

No Account Required

Deep requires no registration, login credentials, or profile creation. All features are immediately available upon installation.

Offline Operation

Deep functions entirely offline. The app does not send data to external servers, use analytics services, collect usage statistics, or share information with third parties.

Data Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored preferences.

Mature Content

Deep contains mature themes and is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Check this page for the most current version.

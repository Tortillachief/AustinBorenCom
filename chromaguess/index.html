Chroma Guess

How well do you really see color? Memorize a shade, then recreate it with HSL sliders. Simple to play, surprisingly hard to master.

Download on the App Store ↗

About

A quick, brain-teasing game about how well you actually see color. You get a few seconds to memorize a random color. Then it disappears, and you recreate it using three sliders: hue, saturation, and lightness. The closer your guess, the higher your score.

That's the whole game. But picking the right shade from memory is a lot harder than it sounds.

  • Pure HSL sliders — no presets, no cheating
  • Adjustable difficulty and memorize time
  • Accessibility-friendly text sizes (S / M / L / XL)
  • Haptic feedback you can toggle off
  • Works offline. No accounts, no ads, no tracking.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Chroma Guess handles information when you use our iOS application.

No Data Collection

Chroma Guess does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data generated by your use of the app remains exclusively on your device.

Offline Operation

Chroma Guess functions entirely offline. The app does not send data to external servers, use analytics services, collect usage statistics, or share information with third parties. All game assets, including fonts, are bundled within the app.

Local Storage Only

Game preferences and session state are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

No Account Required

Chroma Guess requires no registration, login credentials, or profile creation. All features are immediately available upon installation.

Safe for All Ages

Because no data is collected, Chroma Guess is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, we will update the "Last updated" date above and note the change in the app's release notes.
